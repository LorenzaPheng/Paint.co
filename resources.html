Where to display your art (NZ)

  • Depot Artspace (Auckland) - youth-friendly exhibitions
  • Toi Pōneke (Wellington) - open calls for local artists
  • Art by the Sea (Devonport) - supports NZ art
  • Your school or library - ask if they’ll display student work
  • Instagram or other social media - easy ways to share your art online

What to buy

Graphite drawing

  • HB, 2B, 4B pencils
  • kneaded eraser
  • blending stump or tissue
  • sketchbook or smooth paper

Watercolour

  • watercolour paper (300gsm)
  • basic paint set
  • round and flat brushes
  • palette and water jar

Acrylic painting

  • acrylic paint starter set
  • canvas board or thick paper
  • flat and round brushes
  • apron or old t-shirt

Famous art museums (NZ)

  • Te Papa (Wellington) - national gallery and cultural museum
  • Auckland Art Gallery - biggest public collection in NZ
  • Christchurch Art Gallery - modern and historic exhibitions
  • Dunedin Public Art Gallery - local and international artists
  • Te Manawa (Palmerston North) – interactive museum blending art,
    science and history exhibits.
